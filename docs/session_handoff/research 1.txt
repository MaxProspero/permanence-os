The Glamsterdam Upgrade (H1 2026) replaces the external MEV-Boost relay with a native protocol mechanism. This removes the "trusted" middleman and moves the auction directly into the consensus layer.
The New 2-Step Block Production:
1. The Commitment (Slot $n$): A Builder submits a signed execution header and a bid to the network. The Proposer (Validator) chooses the highest bid and signs a commitment to use that builder's payload.
2. The Reveal (Slot $n+1$): Once the commitment is on-chain, the Builder reveals the full transaction list (Execution Payload). If the builder fails to reveal it, they lose their stake, and the slot is skipped.
* Key Advantage: Eliminates the risk of "Relay failure" and makes the market permissionless.
* Performance: Paired with EIP-7928 (Block-Level Access Lists), Ethereum now supports parallel transaction verification, pushing throughput toward 10,000 TPS.
