FAQ

Common Questions

What is Ophtxn?

A governed personal intelligence OS. It wraps AI models with persistent memory, autonomous research agents, multi-channel comms, and human approval gates. Not a chatbot — a runtime that compounds context across sessions.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT and Claude are stateless — every session starts fresh. Ophtxn maintains memory, runs multi-agent workflows, enforces spend caps, and gives you audit trails. You keep control; agents do the work.

What is a governed intelligence OS?

Agents plan and act autonomously, but costly or irreversible actions queue for human approval. Every decision is logged with causal context. Governance is architecture, not policy.

Is it free to use?

Runs in no-spend local mode by default. Paid API usage is opt-in with hard spend caps. Core runtime is open source.

Who is it for?

Founders, operators, and solo builders running high-volume execution stacks who need AI leverage without losing control over decisions, budget, or data.
